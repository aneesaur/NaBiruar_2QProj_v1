Origin

NoughtHungry: For Zero Hunger began with simple annual Christmas food donations to people on the streets of Davao City by a group of Filipino high school students. After two years since the beginning of their tradition, the group decided to upgrade from once-a-year gifts of homemade food packages to bigger operations, such as regular food drives. With that, they established NoughtHungry: For Zero Hunger, promoting Zero Hunger online and starting up community outreaches.

By the time the founding members graduated from high school, NoughtHungry: For Zero Hunger had attained traction nationwide, gaining a number of sponsors.The organization was able to expand and reach out farther with its extended resources and capability. Up to the present day, NoughtHungry: For Zero Hunger remains an organization built on compassion and humanity, continuously striving to become better for the sake of the world.

About the Founder

As an actively concerned individual, NoughtHungry: for Zero Hunger is one of the accomplishments in my life that I am most proud of. I have achieved many things, having been a scholar of Philippine Science High School and being a graduate of the Harvard chemistry program, but being able to live speaking up for and acting on a cause I have been looking out for since childhood outshines all of them. I am Aneesa Fareedah B. Biruar, the first founder of NoughtHungry: For Zero Hunger, and I aspire to make an impact for the better of the world.

Let us work together toward a better future, a better world, free of hunger and full of security.
